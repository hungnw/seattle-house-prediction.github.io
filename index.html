Waka Waka Seattle House Price Prediction



Welcome to the Seattle House Price Prediction website. We want to predict Seattle metropolitan home prices using two different machine learning models: random forest and linear regression. We used data from Seattle home sales during the 2014-2015 time period. After examining and cleaning the Seattle home data, we noticed several sections that could potentially affect the accuracy of our machine learningmodels. A couple of examples of information that can affect the accuracy of our models include data showing whether the home was renovated in addition to when the home was built, and a numeric scale to show the value of the home's view. After taking this into consideration we decided that a successful prediction model should have at least a 75% accuracy reading.

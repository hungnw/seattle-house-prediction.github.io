Predicting Seattle Metropolitan Area Home Prices With Machine Learning

Team Waka Waka



Welcome to the Seattle House Price Prediction website!

We want to predict Seattle metropolitan home prices using two different machine learning models: Random Forest and Linear Regression.

We used data from Seattle home sales during the May2014 - May2015 time period. After examining and cleaning the Seattle home data, we noticed several sections that could potentially affect the accuracy of our machine learning models. A couple of examples of information that can affect the accuracy of our models include data showing whether the home was renovated in addition to when the home was built, and a numeric scale to show the value of the home's view.

After taking this into consideration we decided that a successful prediction model should have at least a 75% accuracy reading.
